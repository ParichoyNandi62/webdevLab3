Location

By Train

Direct Trains: Habra is well-connected by the Indian Railways. You can take a local train or express train from Kolkata to Habra. The station is part of the Sealdah railway division.

By Bus

State Transport Buses: West Bengal State Transport Corporation (WBSTC) and other private operators provide bus services connecting Kolkata and Habra. Buses can be boarded from major terminals like Esplanade or other bus depots in Kolkata.

By Car

Personal Vehicle: Driving to Habra from Kolkata is a convenient option. The distance is approximately 30-40 kilometers, and the drive typically takes around 1 to 1.5 hours, depending on traffic.
